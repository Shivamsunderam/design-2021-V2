About Me

Hello! This is Shivam and I’m a user-centric, growth-mindset, inter-disciplinary designer who thrives in a fast-paced, collaborative environment.

Currently, I’m holding responsibility as a Senior Product Designer at Sprinklr. When I joined Sprinklr the design team was quite small (now have outgrown to 2x) which provided me with an opportunity to overlook high stake products. I played a crucial role in shipping the Sprinklr Growth App and worked across Modern Engagement - Publisher, Dashboard; Modern Research - Stories, Reporting; Modern Advertising - Ads Manager and Platform - Onboarding, Frameworks. The app was a huge success and saw a 3x increment in user adoption within 1 month. After its launch, I worked on reimagining Sprinklr Lite - targeted for SMB and Sprinklr Extend from scratch. Sprinklr Lite turned out to be yet another successful product and emerged as a leader in Forrester Wave, Sales Social Engagement Solutions Q1 2021.

Before Sprinklr, I had a stint at Microsoft which profoundly impacted my craftsmanship skills. Academically I have graduated from the Indian Institute of Technology, Guwahati (IITG) with a bachelor’s in design. I have also represented IITG at an International level after being selected as a Top 5 Student Finalist in the World Usability Congress held in Graz, Austria.

Besides work, I love to take a long stroll in nature. I have recently developed a profound interest in books and have been chasing my target to read 30 books this year.
